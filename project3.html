PROJECT NAME: TACO CONTAINER FOR RESTAURANTS

This project forms part for a contest a spanish restaurant organized to design 3 different containers for dishes they prepared, one of them were "Tacos". I decided to do this design to encourage the consumer to hold and eat the tacos the original way. The design consists is two different parts: the ceramic part in which you place the taco and it helps you eat it, and the wooden one as a holder for the whole plate.
