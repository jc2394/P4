PROJECT NAME: BONNEVILLE

This is a CAD project made in Catia V5, intented to improve Computer Assisted Design skills, it has several parts of a motorcycle all assembled together and fixed. This motorcycle is inspired in the Triumph Bonneville T120, which the designer likes and wanted to recreate. This was a final project for the class taken in Spain: "Computer Aided Design 2", and was graded 2 years ago
